AquariumSystems Durban - Who are we?

We're Aquarium Systems, and we've been building and maintaining aquariums in Durban, South Africa for over 20 years.

We specialize in the construction and maintenance of both saltwater and freshwater aquariums to create a living piece of beautiful art for our customers to enjoy and proudly show off to their friends and family. We produce our aquariums locally and source only the finest equipment and livestock from around the world to bring you the best possible quality and service.

At Aquarium Systems, we believe in innovative ideas for aquarium design and maintainability, always on the lookout for new solutions to tank maintenance and construction, making it more efficient, cheaper but above all else improving the quality of our service to the customer.

Whatever aquarium size or shape your heart desires, give us a call now, so we can make that desire become a reality!

What We Do

Aquarium Construction
Our aquariums are built right here in our warehouse with 100% locally sourced materials. We strive for the utmost quality for our builds so that they may last our customers well into the future.

Have a dream tank in mind? Let us make that dream a reality!
Livestock
We don't use fake corals and rocks for our aquariums, we only use the real deal!

Whether you are looking for new fish, live rock or corals, Aquarium Systems has you covered!
We import our livestock from all over the world, ensuring a diverse range for our customers to choose from to add to their aquariums.
Equipment
We only use and recommend top quality brands for your tank's beating heart. All equipment is handled and installed by our experienced staff during your aquarium installation. Once installed you can rely on us to keep your equipment running like brand new.
Maintenance
We specialize in aquarium maintenance and pride ourselves in exceptional service and are more than happy to give you a beautiful piece of living art. Already have an aquarium? Allow us to keep it running efficiently and optimally.
